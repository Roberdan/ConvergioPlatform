## 5. Human-in-the-Loop Requirement

ConvergioPlatform is designed to assist human decision-making, not replace it. **All critical
decisions — including but not limited to financial transactions, legal actions, medical treatment,
security configurations, and deployment to production — require explicit human oversight and
approval.**

The software provides Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) controls. Users are responsible for:

- Configuring appropriate approval gates for their risk tolerance
- Reviewing agent plans before autonomous execution
- Monitoring agent actions in real time
- Maintaining the ability to pause, override, or terminate agent operations at any time

Disabling or bypassing HITL controls transfers all risk and liability to the operator.

## 7. Data Processing and GDPR

ConvergioPlatform is designed with data minimization as a default principle:

- **Local-first**: User data is processed and stored locally by default.
- **No PII collection**: The platform does not collect, transmit, or store personally identifiable

- **Third-party API calls**: When agents interact with external APIs (e.g., Anthropic Claude,
  GitHub), data is transmitted to those services under their respective privacy policies. Users

opt-in only.
